About Me

I am a Health and Development Economist with over 10 years of experience in health policy analysis and financing across multiple African countries. My expertise spans quantitative and qualitative analysis, cost-effectiveness evaluations, and policy design to advance Universal Health Coverage, gender-responsive financing, and adolescent health outcomes. I currently serve as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the University of Cape Town.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity
This live 8-week course introduces participants to no-code AI tools to automate work processes in research and beyond. Ideal for researchers, students, and professionals in the health and life sciences.

  • Automate document, spreadsheet, and data workflows
  • Organize research and literature with AI
  • Build custom chatbots for your projects
  • Create scientific visuals and presentations
  • Apply skills in a hands-on capstone project
  • Live workshops, help sessions, and community forums
